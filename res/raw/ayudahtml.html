How to play

To start a game, you just need to click on Play in main menu and start having fun.

Rules

This guide is written just for beginners, once you start playing, you won't need anything more than your own intuition.

Goal

In ProyectoFinal you should draw the symbol that appears on the screen before they desappear at the bottom of the screen. Remember that you only can solve the item that is nearest to the bottom, so you can't solve any new one until all other symbol of the screen are solved.

Levels

The game starts with the same falling speed everytime, but this speed is increased when you achieve 100, 500 and 1000 points

Lives

The game start with 3 lives. You lose one each time a symbol is not achieved and it goes until the botton of the screen

Shakes

In addition to lives, if a symbol will be imposible to you, you can take it away without losing any life just shaking your device. When starting, you have 5 shakes; but you will achieve an extra shake each time you get 100 points
